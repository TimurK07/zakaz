    THE CRAFTSPEOPLE

    Craftspeople are skilled artisans who create various items for the community using traditional methods and materials. In the Abantu tribe, craftspeople play a crucial role in the community as they are responsible for creating a wide range of items that are essential to everyday life.
     

    The craftspeople in the Abantu tribe use a variety of materials, including wood, clay, and fibers from plants, to create items such as pottery, baskets, tools, and weapons. They use traditional methods that have been passed down through generations, often incorporating intricate designs and patterns that have cultural significance.
